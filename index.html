About Us


Master Express Garage Doors is owned and operated by Licensed Contractor Gus Beltrame.

Gus grew up in Brazil and legally immigrated to the United States at an early age with his family. He always enjoyed working with his hands. He mastered English quickly and graduated from high school. He became interested in business and entrepreneurship and went on to obtain his Bachelors Degree in Finance from California State University Northridge. He self financed his undergraduate degree by working in construction and decided to continue his career as a businessman in the garage door installation and repair profession.

He initially subcontracted for another licensed professional. Later he studied long and hard to obtain his own required California contractors license and established his own business Master Express Garage Doors.

Since Gus entered into the garage door installation.

Products & Services



Master Express Garage Doors sources its garage door and gate related products from local wholesale distributors and uses USA made products whenever possible.

Master Express Garage Doors supplies the following services:

  • Garage Doors (Steel & Glass)
  • Garage Door Panels
  • Garage Door Openers (Battery Backup Style)
  • Gate Operators
  • Garage Door Opener Wall Switches
  • Garage Door Optical Obstruction Safety Sensors
  • Garage Door & Gate Transmitter Programming
  • Garage Door Tension Springs
  • Garage Door Drums/Cables
  • Garage Door Rollers
  • Garage Door Side Tracks
  • Garage Door Bottom Seal Frames
  • Garage Door Weather Seals
  • Moldings
